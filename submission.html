Scope



Submissions are warmly invited for research papers describing their latest findings and research work to the conference based on the theme. Other related and emerging themes and topics are also welcome.

Paper Submisiion



Prospective authors are invited to submit manuscripts reporting original, unpublished research and recent developments in the topics related to the conference. It is required that the manuscript follows the camera-ready format (double column, 10-point font, maximum of 6 pages, additional 3 pages are allowed at an extra charge). Submissions must include title, abstract, keywords, author and affiliation with email address. All submissions must be in letter-sized PDF. Please double check the paper size in your page setup to make sure you are using the letter-size paper layout (8.5 inch X 11 inch). The paper should not contain page numbers or any special headers or footers.

Paper Submission guidelines


➢ All papers must be submitted online via Conference Management Toolkit(CMT).
➢ Authors are invited to submit 4–6 page manuscript in double-column IEEE format which are relevant to the theme of the conference.
➢Manuscripts must be written in English. The instructions and guidelines of IEEE must be followed as mentioned in the following links.
 •Manuscript templates are located at https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html
 •Plagiarism information at https://www.ieee.org/publications/rights/author-rights-responsibilities.html
 • Copy Right Information at https://www.ieee.org/publications/rights/copyright-policy.html
➢Only Electronic submission in PDF format will be accepted.
➢ Since the review process is double-blinded, the manuscript should not contain any author name(s), affiliations, e-mail ids. The specified space needs to be blank. After the manuscript is accepted, the author names, affiliations, e-mail ids can be incorporated in the camera-ready paper.
➢All paper submissions will be carefully reviewed by at least two/three experts and reviews will be returned to the author(s) with comments to ensure the high quality of the accepted papers.
➢The authors of accepted papers must guarantee that their papers will be presented at the conference and register for conference. Submit only the original material where copyright of all parts is owned by the authors declared and which is not currently under review elsewhere in any form.
