Call For Papers


ICAIT invites soliciting original, previously unpublished and high quality research papers.All the accepted and presented papers will be submitted to IEEE for potential inclusion in the Xplore Digital library. ICAIT takes plagiarism very seriously as a professional misconduct. All papers will be screened for plagiarism and when identified, paper will be rereferred. ICAIT doesn't encourage withdrawals after the paper is  being accepted.


  NOTE:

    Plagiarism policy as per IEEE norms.


 CONFERENCE WEBSITE: https://icait.in


THE TOPICS OF THE CONFERENCE INCLUDE, BUT ARE NOT LIMITED TO:


  Track 1: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.


  Track 2: Data Mining and Big Data Analytics.


  Track 3: Computer Networking.


  Track 4: Applications of Information Technology.


  Track 5: Advances in Aerospace Engineering.
